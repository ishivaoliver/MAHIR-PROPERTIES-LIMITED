About Mahir Properties Limited

Our History

Mahir Properties Limited was founded in 2005 with the vision to transform the real estate industry in Kenya. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted real estate and construction company, helping hundreds of families and businesses find their perfect properties. We pride ourselves on delivering quality services and maintaining lasting relationships with our clients.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide exceptional real estate solutions with honesty, integrity, and commitment. We aim to guide our clients through every step of their real estate journey, ensuring satisfaction and success. Our core values are customer focus, transparency, innovation, and excellence.
